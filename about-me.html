About Me

Hi! I am Ahish Deshpande, a junior at IIIT-Hyderabad pursuing B.Tech (Hons.) in ECE. I was born in Nagpur, India and have lived my entire life in Dubai, UAE, where I did my schooling at the Indian High School. I am extremely interested in Electronics and Computer Science, and can be found on my laptop at most times of the day (and night). Apart from this, I also love playing sports and the piano, amongst other things.

My Hobbies

Cricket

I have been playing cricket for the past 14 years and have been part of my school's cricket team. I have also represented my club in tours to the UK and Mumbai. Apart from cricket, I enjoy football, swimming, tennis and motor sports.

Keyboard

The keyboard/piano is my go to instrument whenever I'm stressed or tensed. I have been playing it since the age of 6. My favorite pieces are Yiruma's River Flows in You and Kiss the Rain.

Design

I am very interested in graphics design and have been tinkering around with the Adobe Creative Suite since 6th grade. I also like to draw and paint on paper once in a while in my free time.

Photography

The methodical and creative ways in which a camera can be used to capture moments has always amazed me, and hence I make sure to carry my camera with me almost everywhere I go :D

Travel

I love to visit and experience different places in the world. More than anything, it helps me appreciate different perspectives and explore different traditions and cultures.
